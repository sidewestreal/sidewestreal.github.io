Mathematical Lemmas, Problems & Solutions

Lemma Example

If $a, b \in \mathbb{R}$, then $a^2 + b^2 \geq 0$.

Problem Example

Prove that for all $x \in \mathbb{R}$, $x^2 \geq 0$.

Solution Example

Since $x^2 = x \cdot x$ and the product of two real numbers with the same sign is non-negative, $x^2 \geq 0$ for all $x \in \mathbb{R}$.

Quadratic reciprocity

For odd distinct primes $p$ and $q$, the following holds: $$\left(\frac pq \right) \left( \frac qp \right) = (-1)^{\frac{(p-1)(q-1)}{4}} $$
